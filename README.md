# Coffee Sales Analysis Project - Sharon Mathurti 

##Project Description: This project analyzes coffee sales data to uncover trends, patterns, and insights that can help improve business decisions. 
The analysis focuses on sales performance across products, days, and customer preferences. 

##Dataset: 
The dataset contains coffee sales transactions including:
Sale ID 
Product Name
Quantity Sold 
Sales Amount 
Transaction Date

##Objective: The main objective of this project is to identify top-selling coffee products.
           Analyze sales trends by weekday.
           Discover actionable insights to optimize sales and inventory. 

##Tools and Technologies Used:

SQL - Data extraction and manipulation.
[Coffee_Sales_Portfolio.xlsx](https://github.com/user-attachments/files/22296051/Coffee_Sales_Portfolio.xlsx)
Excel - Data cleaning, pivot table, and visualization. 

##Analysis and Key insights:
Top-selling products - Latte and Americano with Milk contributed the most of total sales.
Sales by day of the week - Tuesday, Monday, and Friday had the highest sales, while Sundays had the lowest.
Transaction trends - The majority of sales are in small-to-medium quantity transactions, indicating steady demand.
Revenue insights - Some products generate higher revenue despite lower quantity sold, showing premium pricing impact. 

##Conclusion and Recommendations:
Focus on stocking and promoting top-selling products to maximize revenue.
Offer promotions on slower sales days (like Sundays) to boost overall sales. 
Consider analyzing customer preferences further to introduce new flavors or packages.
